Lost Hero

The Lost Hero is a game idea I have been thinking of making. It is set in medevial times. It is about a young hero where is family is killed at a young age and the killers are looking for him, but he has escaped with the help of another family. The hero has no memory of this event until he is older. The hero then goes around the world recruting friends along the way to help him in his quest to avenge his family.This is an opne world RPG game where throught the store the player will be able to make their own decisions on how the story should go and that will efect the outcome at the end.The player will use swords,catipults, and battering rams for wepons
